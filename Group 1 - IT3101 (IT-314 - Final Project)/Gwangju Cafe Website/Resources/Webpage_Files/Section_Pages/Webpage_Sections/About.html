About Us

Welcome to Gwangju Cafe, a proudly local coffee shop rooted in the warm and welcoming community of Dulangan, San Luis, Batangas. What began as a simple idea—to bring good coffee and good energy closer to home—has grown into a beloved neighborhood spot where friends, families, and travelers gather to relax, connect, and enjoy the moment.


Our Story:

Gwangju Cafe was built with one vision: to create a cozy and uplifting space where everyone can enjoy quality drinks and treats without needing to travel far.Inspired by the comforting cafe culture found in Korea and paired with the genuine hospitality of Batanguenos, our shop represents the best of both worlds—familiar, friendly, and always comforting.



What We Offer:

At Gwangju Cafe, we take pride in serving freshly prepared beverages and snacks that make every visit special. Whether you’re craving a classic iced latte, a refreshing fruit drink, or a sweet pick-me-up, we’ve got something for everyone.

We also offer Coffee Cart Services for events and celebrations. From birthdays to community gatherings, our mobile setup brings the Gwangju Cafe experience directly to you—fresh, fun, and convenient.



Our Space:

Located near the basketball court in Dulangan, our cafe embraces the charm of small-town life. We’re a simple, approachable spot where you can unwind, chat with friends, or enjoy your drink while watching the day go by.

Our Commitment:

As a proudly local business, we are dedicated to:
1. Serving consistently great-tasting beverages
2. Providing friendly and heartfelt customer service
3. Supporting and staying connected to our community
4. Growing with San Luis by creating a place people genuinely love to visit


Why “Gwangju”?

Our name is inspired by the lively and artistic spirit of Gwangju in South Korea—a place known for culture, creativity, and heartfelt connections. We bring that same energy to our corner of Batangas, offering a cafe experience that feels unique yet familiar.


Thank You for Supporting Local!

Every cup you enjoy helps us continue our mission of bringing joy, flavor, and community together. We’re grateful for every customer who steps through our doors or invites us to be part of their events.
